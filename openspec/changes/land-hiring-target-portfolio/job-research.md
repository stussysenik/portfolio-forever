# Job Research

Date of synthesis: 2026-04-22

## Primary clusters

### Design engineering

- Ramp Design Engineer: code-first prototyping, React/TypeScript, AI-native workflow, trust-heavy interfaces, accessibility, performance.
- Windmill Founding Design Engineer: end-to-end ownership, AI chat UX, Figma + production code, responsive layouts, motion, metrics.
- Jane Street Front End Software Engineer: expert-user tools, typed functional thinking, elegant and highly usable browser UIs, abstractions and libraries.

### AI product / experience design

- Linear Senior / Staff Product Designer: tight pairing with engineering, prototype in Figma and HTML/CSS, quality as advantage.
- Google DeepMind Staff AI Product Designer roles: AI-native systems thinking, motion, multimodal interfaces, product strategy, system instructions, diverse prototyping methods.
- OpenAI iOS Software Engineer, ChatGPT Engineering: delightful AI product experiences, performance, reliability, end-to-end ownership, mobile quality.

### Frontend systems / platform craft

- Mistral Frontend Engineer: intuitive UX, maintainable code, full-stack collaboration.
- Apple ML / search-adjacent software roles: strong technical rigor, performance, production quality.
- Notion / Basement Studio references: strong product taste, frontend craft, and high-bar execution.

## Portfolio implication

The homepage should not only show projects. It must prove:

- strong visual and interaction craft
- real shipped code, not only mockups
- systems thinking across UI, AI behavior, and content boundaries
- recruiter-friendly routes to inspect evidence quickly

## Public framing rule

Do not pitch to one company by name.

Instead, frame the site to match the shared role bar:

- design engineer
- AI product craft
- frontend systems
